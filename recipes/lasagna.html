Lasagna - A classic Italian dish

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of stacked layers of flat pasta, fillings like ragù (meat sauce), ricotta or béchamel sauce, and melted cheese.

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish made of layered flat pasta alternated with various sauces, cheeses, and meats or vegetables

Ingredients

  • Ground meat: A mix of beef and pork.
  • Aromatics: Finely chopped onion, celery, and carrot.
  • Garlic: Minced fresh cloves.
  • Tomatoes: Tomato paste and crushed canned tomatoes.
  • Liquid: Dry red or white wine and beef stock.
  • Seasoning: Salt, black pepper, and fresh Italian herbs.

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about one minute until fragrant.
  3. Toss in the chopped vegetables, seasoning with salt and pepper. Cook until tender.
  4. Remove from heat, transfer to a serving plate, and garnish with fresh herbs before serving warm.
